Interactive Video

EverythingTag is a unique technology,
which finally makes video content interactive.

You now can give your video viewers a solution to easily discover featured brands, products and other content in a very natural and engaging way, while you can keep your focus on you core product and not the advertisement.

Say 'Good Bye' to the standard video,
banner ads & annoying recommendations.

You can now easily tag any element within your video with good looking & fancy tags. Finally, users will be able to discover all things they like by clicking on the little tags within your videos.

How to use

By mean of our self-service solution you will be able to easily implement beautiful tags into your video. You can choose from a wide range of tag templates and pick up those, which fits the best the style and the content of your video.

Once done, you simply upload your brand new tagged video into your portal, website or blog and enjoy the user engagement and new sales. Our tracking solution allows you to have all stats on user engagement live.
